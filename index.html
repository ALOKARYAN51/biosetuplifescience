Welcome to Biosetup Lifescience

Biosetup Lifescience is opening new doors for learning technologies in life sciences. BSL is an Online LifeScience organization. It provides bountiful information on academics, research areas, careers, funding sources and also guiding for competitive examinations. BSL is taking the opportunity to help students shape their future by imparting knowledge, awareness and career guidance. Now, with a quick search, you will be able to find endless learning opportunities and technologies.

About Us

Biosetup Life sciences is a hub for scientific research and learning. It is approved by ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization and registered under MSME, Govt. of India. BSL comprises a multidisciplinary team of faculty and research associates.
